The 100

It is a book about people who survived the nuclear bomb thing.
Some hid in bunkers and some went to space. Its focused more on the
people who were in space and how they sent 100 teenagers back to earth
to check if the earth had healed and if it was habitable. when they arrived
on the earth in a space shaft they realized that they weren't the only ones
on earth. There were clans such as Azgeda, Trikru, Dessert people etc. later
on they became Wonkru.one nation"you are wonkru or you are the enemy of wonkru"
